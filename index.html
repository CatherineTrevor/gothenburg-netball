Fun, Fitness and Friendship!

Welcome!

We are a group of people who simply love to get together regularly to play netball, and sometimes even go out to enjoy a fika /pint!

Most of us have moved to Sweden from abroad, some never having heard of netball before, but it doesn't matter - everyone is welcome!

Covid update: we are not meeting at the moment, but we hope to start again spring 2021. Make sure to keep coming back here and our Facebook page to check for updates.

How do I join in?

Your first two sessions are free, then if you would like to join, 12 months' membership is 400kr, plus 50kr per session.

We welcome anyone aged 18 or over, regardless your level of fitness or knowledge of netball! Join our next session and give it a go!

What you need to bring...

Indoor training / running shoes

Comfortable clothes for running

A bottle of water
