Welcome!

Set up in 2019, Gothenburg Netball club is a group of people who simply love to get together regularly to play netball and have fun!


We try to play once a week, and sometimes meet for fika or a pint! Each session consists of a warm-up, some exercises, sometimes a review of the rules, including footwork and obstruction - then it's straight into a game!


Most of us have moved to Gothenburg from abroad, some never having heard of netball before, but it doesn't matter - everyone is welcome!

How do I join in?

Your first two sessions are free, then if you would like to join, 12 months' membership is 400kr, plus 50kr per session.


We welcome anyone aged 18 or over: your level of fitness or knowledge of netball doesn't matter to us! Join our next session and give it a go!


What should I bring?

Indoor training / running shoes

Comfortable clothes for running

A bottle of water


Interested in what the players think? Meet our committee members to find out!
