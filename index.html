Welcome to

Gothenburg Netball Club!

Set up in 2019, we are a group of people who simply love to get together regularly to play netball and have fun!

We try to play once a week, and sometimes meet for fika or a pint!

Most of us have moved to Gothenburg from abroad, some never having heard of netball before, but it doesn't matter - everyone is welcome!

Covid update: we are not meeting at the moment, but we hope to start again spring 2021. Visit us here or check out our Facebook page for updates.

What is netball?

Don't know the rules of netball, or it's been so long that you've forgotten? Don't worry, we all help out each other during sessions, but in the meantime check out our quick guide to the game!

How do I join in?

Your first two sessions are free, then if you would like to join, 12 months' membership is 400kr, plus 50kr per session.

We welcome anyone aged 18 or over, regardless your level of fitness or knowledge of netball! Join our next session and give it a go!

What should I bring?

Indoor training / running shoes

Comfortable clothes for running

A bottle of water
